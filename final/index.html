Restaurants in New Orleans, Louisiana

Final Project By: Hannah Kopplin

GEOG 4046

This map displays the locations of restaurants in the City of New Orleans. Each point on the map shows the name of the restaurant and if table service is offered or not. The data came from the NOLA Open Data on ArcGIS Hub that was created in June of 2018. This information can be useful or even life saving in a hurricane or disaster situation.

The state of Louisiana has created an emergency preparedness guide that can be found here which outlines other ways you can protect yourself and your family in the event of a hurricane.
